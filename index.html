Fixed Deposit (FD) Calculator

Calculates maturity amount and interest for a fixed deposit. In India, banks typically compound quarterly. Use the compounding selector if your bank specifies a different frequency.

Recurring Deposit (RD) Calculator

Estimates maturity for monthly deposits. Indian RDs are usually compounded quarterly. We use an equivalent monthly rate derived from quarterly compounding to simulate month-by-month growth.

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)

Computes future value of a monthly mutual fund investment. Choose payment timing: end (ordinary annuity) or start (annuity due) of month.

Lumpsum Investment

Future value of a one-time investment at an expected annual return, compounded monthly.

SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)

Simulates withdrawals from a starting corpus with monthly compounding. Useful for retirement income planning.

Loan EMI (Home / Personal / Car / Education)

Calculates monthly EMI, total interest, and generates an amortization schedule. Works for any installment loan.

NPS Corpus & Pension Estimator

Estimates National Pension System corpus at retirement using yearly contributions (optionally increasing) and assumed returns. Also splits mandatory annuity (min 40%) and estimates monthly pension given an annuity rate.

PPF Maturity Calculator

Approximates Public Provident Fund maturity. Assumes a fixed annual deposit and annual compounding. (Actual PPF interest is calculated monthly on the lower balance between 5th and end; this is a close approximation.)

CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)

Computes the annualized return between a starting value and ending value over a period.

Inflation & Real Returns

Converts a nominal return into a real (inflation-adjusted) return and adjusts future value to today's money.

Goal-based SIP Requirement

Calculates the monthly SIP needed to reach a target corpus, given an expected return and tenure.
